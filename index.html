Known for Quality and Experience

Slowinski Tile was founded in 1995 and has spread by word of mouth. The owner and main tile installer Tony Slowinski is well known as a craftsman of high quality, and a man of integrity. If you aren't sure exactly how you want your project to be done, Tony has the experience to help guide you through it. From concept to finishing touches we are able to do anything needed to assist you in your tile renovation or new construction projects.

Tony has completed thousands of projects over the years from small to large. He has done everything from fireplace installations, showers and backspashes to foot paths, full bathroom tear downs and reinstalls and even the occational tile repair job. His attention to detail and obvious pride in his work is what has endeared him to so many people. Not only is he well known in the broader construction community but by clients and friends of clients as well.

I had tony do tile work for me years ago, and after over a decade one of the tiles came loose after we dropped a cast iron skillet on it. I called him up and even though his schedule is tight he still made time to come take a look. I plan on hiring him for our next renovation!

I knew I wanted our new home to have tile in it but I wasn't sure how I wanted it to look. Tony gave us so many great ideas and even helped us pick our tile design, type and found options for different price points!

I've known Tony for years! Great guy, great work and great family!
